# iZotope Suite

This collection contains a near-complete iZotope plugin ecosystem spanning mastering, mixing, vocal production, audio repair, and creative processing.

---

## Mastering — Ozone

- **Ozone 9 Elements** — Entry-level Ozone with Master Assistant and core modules.
- **Ozone 10** — Full mastering suite with AI Master Assistant, updated modules, and improved workflow.
- **Ozone 11** — Latest generation. Adds the Clarity module for transient/tone separation.

### Ozone Modules (10 & 11)

| Module | Function |
|--------|----------|
| Equalizer | Surgical parametric EQ with dynamic mode |
| Dynamic EQ | Frequency-reactive dynamic equalization |
| Match EQ | Spectral matching to a reference track |
| Dynamics | Full-featured multiband compression |
| Maximizer | Loudness maximization with IRC algorithm |
| Imager | Multiband M/S stereo width control |
| Exciter | Harmonic excitement and saturation |
| Vintage Compressor | Analog-modeled compression |
| Vintage EQ | Analog-modeled passive EQ |
| Vintage Limiter | Analog-modeled limiting |
| Vintage Tape | Tape saturation and coloration |
| Spectral Shaper | Frequency-specific dynamics for resonance taming |
| Stabilizer | Mid/side dynamic stabilization |
| Low End Focus | Targeted low-frequency punch and control |
| Master Rebalance | AI-driven stem-level rebalancing |
| Impact | Transient shaping at mastering stage |
| Clarity *(Ozone 11)* | Transient and tone separation |

### Ozone Standalone Tools

- **Ozone Imager 2** — Free standalone stereo imager and M/S tool.
- **Tonal Balance Control 2** — Cross-plugin spectral reference tool. Connects to Neutron, Nectar, and other iZotope plugins for inter-plugin mix guidance.

---

## Mixing — Neutron

- **Neutron 3 Elements** — Mix assistant with EQ, compressor, transient shaper, and exciter.
- **Neutron 4 Elements** — Updated Neutron with improved AI mix assistance.

---

## Vocal Production — Nectar & VocalSynth

- **Nectar 3 Elements** — Vocal production suite: EQ, compression, harmony, de-esser, pitch correction, and saturation.
- **VocalSynth 2** — Creative vocal instrument and FX: vocoder, talk box, biovox, compuvox, polyvox modes.
- **VocalSynth Pro** — Expanded VocalSynth with additional synthesis modes and routing.

---

## Audio Repair — RX 8

- **RX 8 De-click** — Removes clicks, crackle, and digital artifacts from audio recordings.
- **RX 8 De-clip** — Repairs clipped and over-saturated audio, recovering transient detail.
- **RX 8 De-hum** — Removes electrical hum and harmonic buzz (50/60 Hz and harmonics).
- **RX 8 Voice De-noise** — AI-powered voice noise reduction for clean dialog, vocals, and speech.

---

## Creative & Spectral

- **iZotope Iris 2** — Spectral sampling synthesizer. Draw selections directly on a spectrogram to create tonal, textural, and experimental instruments from any audio.
- **iZotope Trash 2** — Multi-stage distortion and mangling. Waveshaping, convolution, filtering, and more.
- **Vinyl** — Vinyl record simulation with dust, scratch, warp, and electrical noise artifacts.

---

## Utility

- **Relay / iZRelay** — Inter-plugin communication and gain staging. Part of iZotope's inter-plugin communication system.
- **Carve EQ** *(component)* — Spectral EQ tool.
