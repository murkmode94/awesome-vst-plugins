# Valhalla DSP

ValhallaReverbDSP makes some of the most respected reverb and delay plugins available. Every plugin in the Valhalla lineup is in this collection.

---

## Reverb

- **ValhallaRoom** — General-purpose algorithmic room reverb. Smooth, dense, highly usable on virtually any source.
- **ValhallaVintageVerb** — Vintage algorithmic reverb. Emulates the character and coloration of reverb hardware from the 1970s and 1980s (including Plate, Room, Chamber, Concert Hall, etc.).
- **ValhallaShimmer** — Pitch-shifted shimmer reverb. Creates endless, drifting, ethereal pad textures. No other plugin sounds quite like it.
- **ValhallaPlate** — Plate reverb with warm, classic character. Studio-standard sound for vocals, drums, and instruments.
- **ValhallaSupermassive** — **Free.** Massive reverb and delay with extreme decay times. Designed for ambient, drone, and experimental processing. One of the best free plugins ever released.
- **ValhallaFutureVerb** — *(if present in collection)* — Reverb with forward-thinking algorithms aimed at modern and future-facing production aesthetics.

## Delay & Modulation

- **ValhallaDelay** — Valhalla's flagship delay plugin. Tape, HiFi, pitch shift, diffusion, old, bbd, ghost, and more modes. Extensive modulation routing.
- **ValhallaFreqEcho** — Frequency-shifting delay. Combines Bode-style frequency shifting with echo for psychedelic, pitch-smearing, and alien effects.
- **ValhallaUberMod** — Modulation-forward processor. Covers chorus, flanger, ensemble, phaser, multi-tap modulation, and more.
- **ValhallaSpaceModulator** — Flanging taken to extremes. Rich, dense, and spatial — useful from subtle thickening to full flanger chaos.
