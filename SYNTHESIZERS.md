# Synthesizers

A deep dive into every synthesizer plugin in this collection.

---

## Wavetable Synthesizers

- **Serum** — Xfer Records' definitive wavetable synthesizer. Custom wavetable import, high-quality resampling, built-in FX chain, and visual modulation routing. The most-used synth in modern electronic production.
- **Serum 2** — Next-generation Serum with expanded wavetable engine and updated modulation system.
- **SerumFX** — Serum running as a standalone effects chain, making all of Serum's FX available without a note playing.
- **Vital** — Free advanced wavetable synth with spectral warping, complex modulation matrix, and built-in oscilloscope. Serious Serum competition.
- **Massive** — Native Instruments' 2007 wavetable synth. Still a standard for bass, leads, and aggressive sound design.

## Modular & Semi-Modular

- **Phase Plant** — Kilohearts modular softsynth built around their snap-in system. Build your own signal chain from generators, modulators, and effects.

## FM Synthesis

- **Halogen FM** — FM synthesis with classic DX-style architecture.

## Physical Modeling & Organic

- **Synplant** — Sonic Charge's generative synthesizer. Grow sounds from a seed and mutate them organically. Unique approach with no traditional programming required.
- **Equator2** — ROLI's MPE-enabled synthesizer for Seaboard and Lightpad controllers. Polyphonic expression per note: pitch, pressure, slide, lift, strike.

## Hybrid & Character Synths

- **Kult** — Dark experimental synthesizer. Unusual architecture, designed for gritty and unconventional textures.
- **USYNTH** — Streamlined synthesizer focused on fast, ready-to-use sound design.
- **Freak** — Output's synthesizer built around dynamic motion and generative movement.
- **Xpander** — Expansion and synthesis tool.

## Vocal Synthesis

- **VocalSynth 2 / VocalSynth Pro** — iZotope's vocal synthesis instrument. Modes include vocoder, talk box, biovox, compuvox, and polyvox. Transforms voices and instruments into synthetic vocal textures.
